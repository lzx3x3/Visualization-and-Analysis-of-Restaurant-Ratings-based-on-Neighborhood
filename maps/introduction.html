Introduction

   This website is dedicate to provide users with more trustful ratings and costomized choosing restaurants method. The website is designed for users who want to find a satisified restaurants in Atlanta.

How to Use

  When users click the home button, they can see the homepage. The homepage contains two sections. One is a main plot another is a subplot which is under the main plot.

  For the main plot, users can click neighborhoods to gain more information about the neighborhoods. Users will find the average ratings, restaurants numbers and top categories from the left pannel (information pannel). Users can also move their mouses on the circles which denote restaurant on the map. The information pannel will also show more information for selected restaurant. Circles are with different color and size. Circles for higher ratings restaurants are with dark colors. Circles for higher price restaurants are with larger size.

  For the sub plot, users click bars in barchart to choose their favorite categories. Users click coins to choose their acceptable price range. Users drag sliders to choose their prefered ratings range. After choosing, the restaurants which fit the conditions will show in the main plot.
